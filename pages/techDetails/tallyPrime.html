Course Overview

This Tally Prime course is designed for students, commerce learners, and job seekers who want practical accounting software knowledge from the ground up. You will start with company setup, ledgers, and vouchers, then move into inventory, tax processes, and reporting workflows.

The training combines guided practical sessions, assignments, and real transaction exercises so you can confidently record entries, verify reports, and support day-to-day accounting tasks.

  • Practical accounting workflow training
  • Voucher and ledger entry mastery
  • Tax and inventory handling process
  • Reports and reconciliation practice
