Course Overview

This DTP course is designed for students, design learners, and job seekers who want practical publishing and print design skills. You will learn page setup, typography structure, visual composition, and document formatting standards used in real production work.

The training combines guided practical sessions, assignments, and mini projects so you can confidently create publication-ready layouts and print outputs.

  • Structured publishing workflow training
  • Typography and page layout consistency
  • Print-ready output preparation process
  • Project-based assessment and review
