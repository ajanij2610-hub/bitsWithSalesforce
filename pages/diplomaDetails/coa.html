Course Overview

COA certification is mandatory for government jobs through TNPSC exams. The COA course is conducted as per the syllabus approved by the Department of Technical Education (DOTE).

This course consists of four modules:

  • English Typewriting (30wpm)
  • Tamil Typewriting (30wpm)
  • Computer Theory
  • Practical

Program Highlights

  • Computer fundamentals and operating system basics
  • Practical office document creation workflow
  • Spreadsheet formulas, tables, and reporting tasks
  • Presentation preparation and communication support
  • Institute certificate on successful completion

Curriculum Modules
