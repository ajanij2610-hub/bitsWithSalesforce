Diploma Course Details

Diploma in Office Automation and Publishing (DOAP)

DOAP means Diploma in Office Automation and Publishing. This program builds practical skills in office productivity software, document workflow, and publishing-oriented computer applications.

  1. Home

Course Overview

This DOAP course is designed for students, office beginners, and job seekers who want practical automation and publishing skills required in modern workplaces.

The training includes MS Office workflow, document formatting standards, spreadsheet operations, presentation skills, and introductory publishing tools with hands-on lab work.

  • Strong practical focus on daily office automation tasks
  • Publishing-oriented document layout and formatting workflow
  • Structured assignments on productivity and output accuracy
  • Career-oriented preparation for office and data support roles

Program Highlights

  • Professional documentation and formatting practice
  • Spreadsheet operations with formulas and reporting basics
  • Presentation creation and office communication skills
  • Publishing basics and print-ready layout workflow
  • Course completion certificate from institute
