Course Overview

This DCA course is designed for school students, college learners, beginners, and job seekers who want strong computer application skills for academics and office careers.

Training starts from computer fundamentals and keyboard usage, then moves to MS Office, internet/email communication, and practical office file workflow with daily lab sessions.

  • Beginner-friendly training with step-by-step practical guidance
  • Strong focus on Word, Excel, PowerPoint, and digital office workflow
  • Hands-on assignments for typing, formatting, reporting, and data entry
  • Assessment support with certificate-oriented preparation

Program Highlights

  • Computer fundamentals and operating system practice
  • Professional document preparation in MS Word
  • Excel formulas, tables, charts, and data management
  • Presentation design using PowerPoint
  • Course completion certificate from institute
