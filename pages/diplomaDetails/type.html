Course Overview

This typewriting program is designed for students, competitive exam aspirants, and job seekers who need reliable typing speed and accuracy in English and Tamil.

Training includes posture and finger discipline, timed practice drills, error correction methods, and regular speed tests to build confidence in both languages.

  • Systematic training for English and Tamil keyboard layouts
  • Daily speed-building and accuracy improvement drills
  • Timed tests with progress tracking and correction feedback
  • Exam-oriented practice support for typing assessments

Program Highlights

  • Correct finger placement and touch typing technique
  • Speed development through graded exercise sets
  • Accuracy-focused correction and retyping workflow
  • Practical typing training in English and Tamil

Curriculum Modules

Typing Fundamentals
