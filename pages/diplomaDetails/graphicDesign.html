Course Overview

This graphic design course is designed for students, aspiring designers, and job seekers who want practical creative skills for digital and print media projects.

Training covers design fundamentals, color and typography systems, brand-focused layout workflow, and portfolio-oriented project practice with software tools.

  • Hands-on design exercises from concept to final output
  • Strong focus on visual hierarchy, balance, and composition
  • Practical workflow for poster, brochure, and social design assets
  • Portfolio-ready projects for freelance and job interviews

Program Highlights

  • Color theory, design principles, and composition flow
  • Typography basics for branding and readability
  • Creative layout systems for print and digital media
  • Practical design software workflow and output handling
  • Institute certificate after course completion

Curriculum Modules
