Advanced Diploma in Computer Applications (ADCA)

ADCA means Advanced Diploma in Computer Applications. This program gives comprehensive practical training in office productivity, digital workflow, and core application skills.

Course Overview

This ADCA course is designed for students, diploma/degree learners, and job seekers who want broad computer application knowledge with practical classroom and lab-oriented training.

The course covers computer fundamentals, MS Office, internet tools, documentation, data handling, and application-level workflow to prepare learners for modern office roles.

  • Comprehensive computer application training from basics to advanced usage
  • Strong practical focus on productivity and office-ready skills
  • Daily lab practice with assignments and guided activity sheets
  • Career-oriented training support for admin and computer operator roles

Program Highlights

  • Computer concepts and operating system workflow
  • Advanced MS Word and documentation tasks
  • Excel formulas, analysis sheets, and reporting basics
  • Internet, email communication, and digital productivity
  • Certificate on successful course completion
